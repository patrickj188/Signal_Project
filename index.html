We get it, you're busy

When you travel for business, it can feel like you're in a foreign country. There are so many choices when it comes to food, events, and exercise that it can be hard to find just the right thing. You spend all day making tough decisions, and the last thing on your mind is finding the best place to eat or finding the right workout. That's where we come in.

We take the guess work out of finding those perfect places so you can spend your precious down time actually enjoying them.

How it works

Simply enter your name & preferences for food, events, and exercise. Let us handle the rest. In seconds you'll have the very best of each category, right at your fingertips. We'll give you two great options to choose from in each category and directions to each place.

We're obsessed with sorting through the noise to get you out of that hotel room and in to the best places your city has to offer.
